## What are Algebraic Fractions?
Algebraic fractions are expressions where the numerator, denominator, or both are algebraic expressions.

---

## Simplifying Algebraic Fractions

### Steps:
1. Factor both the numerator and denominator completely.
2. Cancel out any common factors.

### Example:
Simplify:

`
(c^2 - 9) / (c^2 - 9)
`

Factor numerator and denominator:

`
 (c + 3)(c - 3) / (c + 3)(c - 3)
`
