## Multiplication and Division of Terms

### Multiplication

Multiply the coefficients and apply the laws of exponents to variables.

**Example:**

`2x × 3x = 6x^2`

### Division

Divide the coefficients and subtract the powers of like bases.

**Example:**

`(6a2)÷(2a)=3a`

✅ Tip: Use the index laws when dealing with powers.
